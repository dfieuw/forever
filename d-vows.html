For Your Eyes Only

My Vows to You

I.

I vow to choose you every single day. Not just on the easy days when we're laughing over dinner or singing along at a concert. On the hard days too. The tired days, the frustrating days, the days when life doesn't look like the version we planned. I will choose you first, every time.

II.

I vow to never stop showing up. The way you show up for me changed what I thought love could be. I promise to match that energy. To be steady. To be present. To never leave you wondering where you stand with me. You will always know.

III.

I vow to bring you coffee every single morning. Even when we're not getting along. Even when you hogged the blankets. Even when I'm annoyed and haven't said why yet. The coffee comes first. Everything else can wait.

IV.

I vow to be your laundry fairy forever. It will just appear, clean and folded, like magic. You will never have to ask. You will never have to wonder. This is the hill I will die on and the one domestic superpower I will never retire.

V.

I vow to protect our Friday nights. No matter how busy life gets. A new restaurant, a couch night, a walk to nowhere. The tradition matters less than the promise behind it: we will never stop dating each other.

VI.

I vow to keep asking. How was your day. What are you feeling. What's your favorite part. What do you need from me. I will never assume I already know. I will keep learning you for the rest of my life, because you are worth paying attention to.

VII.

I vow to try to change the radio when Adele comes on. Try. I said try. No guarantees. Sometimes "Someone Like You" just needs to happen and you're going to have to accept that about me. But I will at least reach for the dial. Most of the time.

VIII.

I vow to be brave because you taught me how. You opened your entire world to me. You let me in when it would have been easier not to. I promise to match your courage. To say the hard things out loud. To never hide from you.

IX.

I vow to love you when you don't feel loveable. You do that for me. You love me through the mess, the doubt, the days I can't see what you see in me. I promise to do the same. You will never have to earn my love. It is yours. It has been yours.

X.

I vow to keep our list growing. More states, more capitals, more countries, more trails, more concerts, more restaurants we can't pronounce. I vow to always say yes when you say "should we try it?" and to never let us get comfortable enough to stop exploring.

XI.

I vow to laugh with you every single day. At comedy shows. At bad reality TV. At ourselves. At the things only we find funny. I will never let a day pass without finding something to laugh about together, because nothing sounds better than you laughing.

XII.

I vow to never stop saying it. I love you. Out loud. Every morning, every night, and at random moments in between when it hits me all over again that I get to do this life with you. You will hear it so often you'll roll your eyes, and I still won't stop.

These are my promises to you. Not just for the wedding day, but for every single day after. You are my forever.

Darci
