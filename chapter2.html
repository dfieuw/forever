The Highlight Reel

Favorite Memories

⛺ Camping

Under the Stars

Campfire smoke in our hair, stars above, your head on my shoulder. These are the moments that remind me life doesn't need to be complicated to be perfect.

🎸 Concerts

Our Soundtrack

Your hand finding mine in the dark. You dancing like nobody's watching. The way you look at me mid-song like we're the only two people there. Every show with you is a core memory.

🎰 Vegas

Vegas Nights

Walking the strip until our feet hurt and doing it again the next night. The city that never sleeps met the couple that never runs out of things to talk about.

🥂 Friday nights

Couch Nights

Love is Blind. Married at First Sight. You never watched reality TV before me and now you're hooked. Cooking dinner together then yelling at the screen together. I corrupted you and I'm not sorry.

🇧🇪 Belgium

Across the Ocean

Following the comic book murals through Brussels like a scavenger hunt. Trying every waffle we could find. You navigating trains in a language you didn't speak like you'd done it your whole life. Seven countries and my favorite view in every single one was you.

🚣 Alpine, WY

Into the Rapids

You rode the bull and didn't fall in. Honestly, that's a metaphor for our whole relationship: you just hold on, look fearless, and somehow make it look easy. Meanwhile I was screaming from the raft.

🏡 Home

The Everyday Magic

Morning coffee. Finding the End of Redwood just because we could. Our nightly "what was your feeling and favorite part of the day?" And yes, "did we take meat out for dinner tomorrow?" (we never did). The quiet, random, everything moments. Those are my favorites.

🌄 Adventure

Trying Everything

You'll say yes to anything. A cooking class, a random trail, a restaurant we can't pronounce. You never make me feel silly for suggesting something new. Our list of "we should try that" keeps growing and I plan to check off every single one with you.
