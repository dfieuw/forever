The Highlight Reel

Favorite Memories

⛺ Camping

Under the Stars

Campfire smoke in our hair, stars above, your head on my shoulder. These are the moments that remind me life doesn't need to be complicated to be perfect.

🎸 Concerts

Our Soundtrack

Your hand finding mine in the dark. You dancing like nobody's watching. The way you look at me mid-song like we're the only two people there. Every show with you is a core memory.

🎰 Vegas

Vegas Nights

Neon lights and late nights. We didn't need to gamble. We already hit the jackpot finding each other.

🥂 Friday nights

Couch Nights

Love is Blind. Married at First Sight. You never watched reality TV before me and now you're hooked. Cooking dinner together then yelling at the screen together. I corrupted you and I'm not sorry.

🇧🇪 Belgium

Across the Ocean

Amsterdam, Brussels, Bruges, Ghent, Antwerp, Luxembourg, Germany. We crammed an entire continent into one trip. Different countries, same feeling. You make the whole world feel like ours.

🚣 Alpine, WY

Into the Rapids

You rode the bull and didn't fall in. Honestly, that's a metaphor for our whole relationship: you just hold on, look fearless, and somehow make it look easy. Meanwhile I was screaming from the raft.

🏡 Home

The Everyday Magic

Morning coffee. Our nightly "what was your feeling and favorite part of the day?" And yes, "did we take meat out for dinner tomorrow?" (we never did). The quiet, ordinary, everything moments. Those are my favorites.

🌄 Adventure

Trying Everything

New restaurants. New trails. New cities. New Reese's flavors. We love trying new things together and we never run out of ideas. The list keeps growing and I wouldn't have it any other way.
