The Highlight Reel

Favorite Memories

⛺ Camping

Under the Stars

Campfire smoke in our hair, stars above, your head on my shoulder. These are the moments that remind me life doesn't need to be complicated to be perfect.

🎸 Concerts

Our Soundtrack

Every concert with you is a core memory. The bass drops, the crowd energy, your hand finding mine in the dark. Music sounds better when you're there.

🎰 Vegas

Vegas Nights

Neon lights and late nights. We didn't need to gamble. We already hit the jackpot finding each other.

🥂 Friday nights

Friday Rituals

Our weekly date nights are proof that romance isn't about grand gestures. It's about showing up, every week, and choosing each other on purpose.

🇧🇪 Belgium

Across the Ocean

Belgium proved what I already knew. I'd go anywhere with you. Different country, same butterflies. You make the whole world feel like home.

🚣 Alpine, WY

Into the Rapids

You rode the bull and didn't fall in. Honestly, that's a metaphor for our whole relationship: you just hold on, look fearless, and somehow make it look easy. Meanwhile I was screaming from the raft.

🏡 Home

The Everyday Magic

Morning coffee. Our nightly "what was your feeling and favorite part of the day?" And yes, "did we take meat out for dinner tomorrow?" (we never did). The quiet, ordinary, everything moments. Those are my favorites.

🌄 Adventure

Every Day Is an Adventure

It doesn't matter if we're exploring a new city, getting lost, or just running errands. Everything feels like an adventure when I'm with you.
