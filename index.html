My Dearest Kris,

I've been trying to find the right words, but the truth is, there aren't enough of them. No sentence is big enough to hold what you mean to me.

From the moment we matched on Facebook Dating (fine, "Marketplace"), something just clicked. Our first date at Cucina, that first kiss. I knew right then that my life was about to change.

You came into my life and quietly made everything better. The way you laugh, the way you love, the way you see me, really see me. Changed everything.

We've walked through snowfall in Park City, floated down rivers in Alpine (and you rode that bull like a champion), crossed oceans to Belgium, licked each other's faces on State Street, you've danced at every concert, and we've built a home together. And through every single moment, I've known one thing with absolute certainty:

You are my person.

I love hearing you laugh. It's my favorite sound. I love our Friday date nights, Slackwater, Cotton Bottom, wherever. I love camping and getting lost and comedy shows and concerts. I love our nightly check-ins, asking about each other's feelings and favorites, and the answer to "did we take meat out?" always being no. I love how you show up. Every single day, without being asked. I love watching you be brave. I love that you chose me.

And now I'm asking you to keep choosing me. Forever.

With all my love, always,

Darci
